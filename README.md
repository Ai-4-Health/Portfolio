🌍 Why We Are Building This
Africa faces a healthcare crisis that data and technology can help solve:

1 doctor serves more than 20,000 patients in many rural Tanzanian districts
Manual record-keeping leads to lost data, repeated tests, and avoidable deaths
Late diagnosis is the norm, not the exception, in underserved communities
No analytical tools exist for small facilities to understand their patient population

We are not building this for a hackathon prize or a grade. We are building it because the communities we come from need it.
